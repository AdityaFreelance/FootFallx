about us

At FootfallX, we are 100% focused on brand activations and powered by the expertise of Janus Koncepts, a leading integrated marketing group. We exist to create real consumer connections — experiences that go beyond traditional advertising and transform into lasting brand love. Unlike general marketing agencies, our specialization lies in crafting on-ground activations that blend creativity, flawless execution, and smart technology. With every campaign, we focus on driving measurable impact, ensuring that brands don’t just make noise, but create memorable experiences backed by data-driven insights.

Our Mission

To redefine brand activations by delivering innovative, tech-enabled experiences that engage audiences, build trust, and generate measurable results — turning every footfall into a meaningful brand connection.
